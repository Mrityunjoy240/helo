# Dr. B.C. Roy Engineering College (BCREC)

## College Overview

**Dr. B.C. Roy Engineering College (BCREC)** is a premier engineering institution located in Durgapur, West Bengal, India. Established in **2000**, the college has grown to become one of the most respected technical institutions in Eastern India.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Established** | 2000 |
| **Location** | Durgapur, West Bengal |
| **Campus Area** | 25 acres |
| **Affiliation** | MAKAUT (Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology) |
| **Accreditation** | NAAC Grade B+, NBA Accredited |
| **Approval** | AICTE Approved |
| **Ranking** | Collegedunia 2025: 440th, NIRF 2025: 201-250 |

## Mission & Vision

BCREC aims to provide quality technical education that prepares students for successful careers in engineering and technology. The college emphasizes both academic excellence and holistic development through practical learning, research, and industry exposure.
