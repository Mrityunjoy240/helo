# BCREC Fee Structure

## B.Tech Fees

### Total Course Fees
The total fees for B.Tech programs at BCREC range from **₹4.49 Lakhs to ₹6.1 Lakhs** depending on the branch and specialization.

### Fee Breakdown (Per Semester)
| Fee Component | Amount |
|---------------|--------|
| Admission Fee | ₹10,000 |
| Tuition Fee | ₹45,000 |
| Development Fee | ₹5,000 |
| Library Fee | ₹1,000 |
| **Total per Semester** | **₹63,250** |

### First Year Fees
- **CSE, IT, ECE, EE**: ₹1.09 Lakhs - ₹1.69 Lakhs
- **Specializations (AIML, Data Science, CSD)**: Similar range based on branch

### Hostel Fees (If Applicable)
| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Boarding per Semester | ₹35,000 |
| Mess Charges (per month) | ₹4,000 |
| Security Deposit | ₹10,000 |
| **Total Hostel (per semester)** | **₹35,000 + ₹4,000/month** |

### Total Cost with Hostel
- **Without Hostel**: ₹5.5 Lakhs (4 years)
- **With Hostel**: ₹7.4 - ₹8.5 Lakhs (4 years)

## Payment Schedule

- **Mode**: Semester-wise payment
- **Methods**: Online payment, Bank draft, Cash at accounts office
- **Due Date**: Within 15 days of semester commencement

---

## Scholarships & Financial Aid

1. **Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW)**: For meritorious students from economically weaker sections
2. **Government Scholarships**: Various state and central scholarships available
3. **Merit Scholarships**: For GATE qualified M.Tech students
4. **EWS Scholarships**: Under economically weaker section schemes

---

## Refund Policy

- Fees are refundable as per AICTE guidelines
- Apply for refund through accounts department
- Processing time: 2-4 weeks after application
