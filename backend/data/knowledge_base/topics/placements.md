## Placement Process

1. **Pre-Placement Talk**: Companies visit campus and give presentations
2. **Application**: Students apply for eligible companies
3. **Shortlisting**: Based on resume, marks, and eligibility criteria
4. **Written Test**: Aptitude/Technical tests
5. **Group Discussion**: For some companies
6. **Personal Interview**: Technical and HR rounds
7. **Result Announcement**: Selected students receive offer letters

## Tips for Students

1. **Maintain Good CGPA**: Minimum 6.0-7.0 for most companies
2. **Build Technical Skills**: Focus on programming, data structures, algorithms
3. **Work on Projects**: Practical experience matters
4. **Prepare for Aptitude**: Practice regularly
5. **Improve Communication**: Essential for interviews
6. **Network**: Connect with alumni for guidance
