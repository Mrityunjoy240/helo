# BCREC Admission Information

## Eligibility Criteria

### B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology)
- **Educational Qualification**: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
- **Minimum Marks**: 50% aggregate for General category
- **Entrance Exams**: WBJEE or JEE Main

### MBA (Master of Business Administration)
- **Educational Qualification**: Bachelor's degree in any discipline
- **Minimum Marks**: 50% aggregate
- **Entrance Exams**: JEMAT, MAT, CMAT, or CAT

### MCA (Master of Computer Applications)
- **Educational Qualification**: Bachelor's degree with Mathematics at 10+2 level
- **Minimum Marks**: 50% aggregate
- **Entrance Exam**: WB-JECA

### M.Tech (Master of Technology)
- **Educational Qualification**: B.E./B.Tech or M.Sc. in relevant discipline
- **Entrance Exam**: GATE or MAKAUT-PGCET

## Admission Process

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Appear for Entrance Exam**
   - Register for WBJEE at wbjeeb.nic.in OR
   - Appear for JEE Main

2. **Participate in Counseling**
   - Register for WBJEEB counseling
   - Fill BCREC as preference
   - Lock your choices

3. **Seat Allotment**
   - Based on your rank and preferences
   - Check allotment result on wbjeeb.nic.in

4. **Document Verification**
   - Visit allotted college with original documents
   - Get documents verified

5. **Pay Fees**
   - Pay admission fees at college
   - Complete enrollment process

6. **Begin Classes**
   - Attend orientation program
   - Start your academic journey
