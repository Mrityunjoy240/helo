# Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

## Program Overview

**B.Tech in Electronics & Communication Engineering** at BCREC focuses on electronic devices, communication systems, and signal processing.

## Key Details

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Duration** | 4 Years (8 Semesters) |
| **Intake** | 120 Students |
| **Affiliation** | MAKAUT |
| **Accreditation** | NBA |

## Eligibility

- 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
- Minimum 50% aggregate marks
- Valid WBJEE or JEE Main score

| General | 33,000 | 40,000 |

## Placement Statistics

- **Placement Rate**: 70-75%
- **Average Package**: ₹4.5-5.5 LPA
- **Highest Package**: ₹9-12 LPA

## Curriculum Overview

### Year 1 (Foundation)
- Engineering Mathematics
- Physics & Chemistry
- C Programming
- Electronic Devices
- Circuit Theory

### Year 2 (Electronics Core)
- Digital Electronics
- Signals & Systems
- Analog Circuits
- Electromagnetic Theory
- Microprocessors

### Year 3 (Communication)
- Communication Systems
- Digital Signal Processing
- VLSI Design
- Microwave Engineering
- Control Systems

### Year 4 (Advanced)
- Capstone Project
- Industrial Training
- Electives
