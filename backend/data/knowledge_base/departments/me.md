# Mechanical Engineering (ME)

## Program Overview

**B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering** at BCREC focuses on design, manufacturing, and thermal engineering.

## Key Details

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Duration** | 4 Years (8 Semesters) |
| **Intake** | 60 Students |
| **Affiliation** | MAKAUT |
| **Accreditation** | NBA |

## Eligibility

- 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
- Minimum 50% aggregate marks
- Valid WBJEE or JEE Main score

- **Placement Rate**: 60-70%

- **Highest Package**: ₹8-10 LPA

## Curriculum Overview

### Year 1 (Foundation)
- Engineering Mathematics
- Physics & Chemistry
- Engineering Graphics
- Workshop Practice
- Basic Mechanical Engineering

### Year 2 (Mechanical Core)
- Strength of Materials
- Thermodynamics
- Fluid Mechanics
- Manufacturing Technology
- Machine Drawing

### Year 3 (Advanced)
- Heat Transfer
- Design of Machine Elements
- Automobile Engineering
- CAD/CAM
- Industrial Engineering

### Year 4 (Specialization)
- Capstone Project
- Industrial Training
- Electives
