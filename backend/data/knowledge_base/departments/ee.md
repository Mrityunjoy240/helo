# Electrical Engineering (EE)

## Program Overview

**B.Tech in Electrical Engineering** at BCREC focuses on power systems, electrical machines, and control systems.

## Key Details

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Duration** | 4 Years (8 Semesters) |
| **Intake** | 120 Students |
| **Affiliation** | MAKAUT |
| **Accreditation** | NBA |

## Eligibility

- 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
- Minimum 50% aggregate marks
- Valid WBJEE or JEE Main score

## Placement Statistics

- **Placement Rate**: 70-75%

- **Highest Package**: ₹8-10 LPA

## Curriculum Overview

### Year 1 (Foundation)
- Engineering Mathematics
- Physics & Chemistry
- C Programming
- Basic Electrical Engineering
- Engineering Mechanics

### Year 2 (Electrical Core)
- Circuit Theory
- Electrical Machines
- Power Systems
- Electromagnetic Fields
- Electronic Devices

### Year 3 (Advanced)
- Power Electronics
- Control Systems
- Instrumentation
- Renewable Energy
- High Voltage Engineering

### Year 4 (Specialization)
- Capstone Project
- Industrial Training
- Electives
